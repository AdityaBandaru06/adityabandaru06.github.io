3. Cyberbullying Detection based on Semantic-Enhanced Marginalized Denoising Auto-Encoder

A social media platform similar to facebook, it detects bullying words when written in comments or posts. Our method named Semantic-Enhanced Marginalized Denoising Auto-Encoder (smSDA) is developed via semantic extension of the popular deep learning model stacked denoising autoencoder.
